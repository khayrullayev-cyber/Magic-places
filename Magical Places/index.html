The North

The North - the continent occupying the central part of the Antarctic South Pole region. The area is 12.4 million square kilometers, together with the ice shelves and the islands connected to the mainland through them, 13.975 million square kilometers.

Zhangjiajie National Forest Park

Discover the surreal landscape of Zhangjiajie National Forest Park, which inspired the movie Avatar, on this 2-day tour and make the most of your time with a local guide.

Delicate Arch

In a park with over 2,000 stone arches, this particular free-standing arch has become a widely recognized symbol of the state of Utah and one of the most famous geologic features in the world.

Hamersley Gorge

Located in the north west corner of Karijini National Park, Hamersley Gorge is full of swirling bands of colour, ancient rock, texture, and reflections.
